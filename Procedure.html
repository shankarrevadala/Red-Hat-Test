Prerequisites

Keep the following handy before preparing tea:

  • steel vessel
  • 50 ml water
  • 100 ml milk
  • tea powder
  • sugar
  • spoon
  • strainer
  • tea cup

Preparing tea

  1. Pour 50 ml water in a vessel and boil for five minutes.
  2. Add 100 ml milk and two spoons of tea powder to the boiling water, and boil for five minutes.
  3. Add required quantity of sugar.
  4. Stir the contents thoroughly with a spoon.
  5. Strain the tea and serve in a tea cup.

    6. You can now relish hot tea.
